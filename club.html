Get in touch!

Join our cozy knitting community and discover the joy of creating handmade treasures.

Step into our knitting club, a place where both beginners and experts find common ground over the clack of needles and the warmth of a freshly brewed cup. Located in Ellesmere Port, we offer a cozy retreat for £4, where enthusiasts from all walks of life gather to share, learn, and grow. Our sessions, steeped in biscuits and hot tea, are the perfect backdrop to explore new patterns or perfect your craft.

Regardless of your skill level, our community is here to welcome you with open arms. Come, join us, and be part of a network where every stitch contributes to the fabric of our friendly circle. Drop by whenever and join the fun.

Reasons to join

Metal Well-being

Joining a knitting club can truly soothe your mind, offering a meaningful creative outlet and a warm sense of community to uplift your overall mental well-being.

Social

Knitting clubs provide a fun and sociable environment where you can enjoy the creative process of knitting while making new friends and sharing experiences

Improve

In a knitting club, the collective creativity and shared tips lead to fresh ideas and improved skills, enhancing your enjoyment and proficiency in your hobby.
