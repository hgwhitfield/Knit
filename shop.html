Welcome to our little knitting nook! We're your friendly local spot tucked away in the Ellesmere Port, where we're all about knitting, chatting, and having a good ol' time. We've got shelves stacked with everything a knitter's heart could desire. Here at Knit and Natter we believe in being the best local spot to get what you want, being life long knitter ourselves we stock everything we know you might ever need.

We supply products from a number of the UK's leading brands and always keep a large stock, and if there's anything you can't find get in touch and we can help source it for you! So pop in, have a brew and a look around, and let's get those needles clacking together!
