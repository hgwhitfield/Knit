Knit & Natter with Jan

Hi, I'm Jan. After years of working in healthcare, where nurturing and care are at the heart of every day, I decided to weave those same values into my own knitting shop and club, Knit and Natter with Jan. As an avid knitter myself, I understand the joy and tranquility that comes from knitting, whether you're casting on for the first time or you're about to bind off a complex pattern. Our doors are open to everyone, from beginners who've never held needles to long-time knitters looking for their next challenge. We pride ourselves on creating a helpful, welcoming environment that's inclusive of all ages. At Knit and Natter with Jan, we're more than just a shop; we're a community where every stitch contributes to the tapestry of our shared passion.
